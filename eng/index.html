ABOUT US

The "Entangled Artists" team is a group that has been producing 2D animations since 2019. We create original films, series as well as short films, but we do not limit ourselves only to original projects, because we are constantly open to external orders.

The group consists of people with various experiences, but the most important thing is that each of us shares the passion for writing complex and extensive stories, film editing and production, animation, etc. The initial goal of the team was to create content "from people to people", so that everyone participating in the project can demonstrate their skills and have fun working on our projects. We are currently aiming for the highest one the quality of production we can achieve, in order to set the bar higher and higher and constantly learn new things.

We operate mainly on YouTube, but we are also active on social media such as LinkedIn, Vimeo and Instagram, where we also publish content related to our work.
