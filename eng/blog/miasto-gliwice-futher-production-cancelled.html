"Miasto Gliwice" further production cancelled

Date: 03.11.2023

  • "Miasto Gliwice" the first series and both the first production of the Entangled Artists group (formerly Red Diamond Studio) were announced in July 2022 in the form of a trailer for the second season.

    At first glance, it was obvious that the next season of the seemingly eagerly developed production would look much better than the first one due to many changes that had taken place, including a change in the program in which the series was to be created, improved animation and new voice actors.

    This would be an almost perfect way to further develop the group's YouTube channel and curry favor with the series fans. So why the decision to end the production of the series and cancel season 2?

    "Miasto Gliwice" is a niche and no-budget animated series inspired and maintained in the style of "South Park". The series had 5 episodes of the first season, which, despite the fact that they were not top-notch, were mostly positively received by the audience.

    After completing the first series of episodes, the creators focused on creating short animations in a completely different, original style. After some time, however, they decided to ignite a glimmer of hope in the channel's viewers for the return of their favorite series.

    When the trailer for the second season was published, comments appeared almost immediately showing satisfaction with the future return to creating the series with improved visuals, fresh and funny adventures of the characters.

    and regularity of film publications. Unfortunately, fans didn't get a second season or regular updates on the channel from the creators, but they could read in the video description that the trailer was just a joke on their part.

    "We finally got down to business and will do something different. This trailer is just a joke on our part and the series itself will most likely never be continued. We finished production right after the premiere of the fourth episode of the first season."

    Reading this description, you may feel a bit disappointed after such a long wait and it turns out that the production you have been waiting for for so long and which even received a trailer of "upcoming" news will never be made.

    • Why this decision?

  • The head of the group and the director of the series explains this state of affairs by explaining that he is fed up with creating imitations based on someone else's work.

    Initially, this series was a joke in itself and I never expected that I would make anything out of it, but I did. Thanks to this project, I actually started my adventure with animation and learned a lot in this field. This does not change the fact that with

    every next episode I felt more and more dislike for what I was creating because it wasn't really an original production in the sense it should be and it was simply a poor imitation of something extra. I just want to create something original,

    something that both me and my group will be associated with and that we can be proud of. From now on we will only focus on original designs and not fakes. - Jakub Wiśniewski

    The end of the production of "Miasto Gliwice" does not mean the end of the group's activities, so what are its further plans for the future?

    In the near future, we will focus on more important projects, including those from third parties, but sooner or later we will return with a strong, compelling film, series or short.
